# Orbit (WIP)

## Introduction

'Orbit' is a small game set 1 billion years in the future. Try to escape earth's gravity well as earth's last sentient being.

## Notes

Material maps:
- Albedo
- Normal
- Metallic roughness (metallic on r, roughness on g)
- AO
- Emissive
